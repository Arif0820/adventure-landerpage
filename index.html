DESERT DASH SXS RIDE

DATES: MARCH 26ST - MARCH 30TH 2025

LOCATION: SAFFORD, AZ

Gear up for the Desert Dash SXS Ride, an all-men’s off-road adventure from San Tan Valley to Safford, Arizona! This exciting journey through Arizona’s rugged desert landscape takes you on a thrilling side-by-side (SXS) ride across diverse terrain, from rocky trails to open desert stretches, with breathtaking views along the way. Connect with fellow riders, test your skills, and build camaraderie through shared challenges and experiences on this unique cross-desert trek. Enjoy the thrill of the ride by day and unwind with fireside gatherings under the stars each night. Desert Dash is your chance to break free, push boundaries, and make memories in Arizona’s vast desert expanse.

BOATS & BASS

DATES: OCT 15TH - OCT 19TH 2025

LOCATION: LAKE POWELL, AZ

Escape to Valhalla on the waters of Lake Powell for Boats & Bass, a men’s experience focused on adventure, relaxation, and genuine connection. Spend your days on the water, fishing, boating, and exploring the scenic canyons, while evenings bring powerful group discussions, campfires, and camaraderie under the stars. This experience is a chance to break from routine, recharge, and reconnect with yourself and other men in the great outdoors. Whether you’re casting a line, cruising the lake, or sharing stories by the fire, Boats & Bass offers a unique experience of brotherhood, personal growth, and unforgettable memories.

Valhalla

[ vahl-hah-luh ]

In modern life, "Valhalla" can symbolize a state of ultimate fulfillment and honor, where individuals seek to celebrate their achievements, camaraderie, and the legacies they leave behind. It represents a sanctuary for those who have faced challenges and adversity, encouraging people to live fully and authentically, fostering connections and shared experiences with others as they navigate their own battles in life
